Visualization of Christmas Lyrics

Joseph Fallait, Andres Fernandez, and Yun Tong

Christmas Music has remained a hallmark of the holiday season, with the songs each year often having similar features and lyrics. Done as a part of the Visualization course at Boston College, this webpage used D3 to create some novel visualizations of the Billboard Holiday Charts and of the lyrical makeup of many of these popular classics.
